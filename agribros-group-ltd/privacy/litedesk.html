Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2025

Lite Desk respects your privacy. We collect no personal data unless given explicitly. Downloads and media analysis occur locally. Device IDs may be used for analytics internally; nothing is shared with third parties. Data is encrypted in transit. You may request deletion by contacting us.

  • Firebase Analytics / AdMob: anonymous ad and usage metrics
